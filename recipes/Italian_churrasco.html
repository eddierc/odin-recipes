Italian Churrasco


Description

Not Italian at all!! Amazing Chilean Sandwich.
ideal for the evening with a beer.

Ingredients:

  • Frica bread (can be other similar kinds also)
  • Tomato
  • Avocado
  • Home made mayonaise (add garlic for perfect flavor)
  • Thin sliced cow meat

Steps

  1. Fry the meat in a very hot pan
  2. Cut the tomato into slices
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth
  4. Put the meat on top of the bottom part of the bread
  5. Add the tomato, the avocado and the mayonnaise
