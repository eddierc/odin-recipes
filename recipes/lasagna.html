Lasagna Bolognese


Description

A classical italian recipe, known worldwide.
The best meal for every weekend.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta for lasagna
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ham
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Tomato Sauce
  2. Take a slice of pasta and soak it in water
  3. Put the pasta into a recipe and add the cheese, ham, and socue
  4. Repeat until the lasagna get the required size
  5. Place into the oven at 380F for 40 minutes
